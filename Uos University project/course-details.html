Computer Science

          This course offers a comprehensive overview of computer science,
          covering topics such as algorithms, data structures, software
          engineering, and more. Students will gain hands-on experience through
          projects and internships, preparing them for careers in software
          development, cybersecurity, and IT management.
        
          Duration: 3 years
          Entry Requirements: A-levels in Mathematics and Physics or equivalent
          qualifications.
