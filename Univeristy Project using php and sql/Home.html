FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What programs does the University of stirling offer?▼
A: The University of stirling offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines including business, engineering, health, humanities, and sciences.
Q: How do I apply for admission?▼
A: You can apply for admission online through our website. Please visit the admissions section for detailed instructions and application deadlines.
Q: Can international students apply?▼
A: Yes, international students are welcome to apply. Please refer to the international admissions section on our website for specific requirements and application procedures for international applicants.
Q: What are the tuition fees?▼
A: Tuition fees vary depending on the program of study and your residency status. Please refer to the tuition fees section on our website for current rates.
Q: Are scholarships available?▼
A: Yes, the University of stirling offers scholarships and financial aid to eligible students. Please visit the scholarships and bursaries section on our website for more information.
Q: How can I contact student services?▼
A: You can contact student services by phone, email, or in person. Visit the student services section on our website for contact information and office hours.
